Lower prices for 4GB and 8GB Compute Module 4

Written By Daniel Morrisey May 8, 2025

News

"At Raspberry Pi, our mission is to make computing accessible and affordable for everyone and for businesses at every scale"-Dave Lee

Raspberry Pi announced today that purchasing a Compute Module 4 through an authorized Raspberry Pi reseller will grant you a $5 reduction on the 4GB RAM version and a $10 discount on the 8GB RAM variant.

The Raspberry Pi Compute Module 4 is vital for a wide range of uses, from healthcare equipment to entertainment systems. Many endeavors do not require the Compute Module 5's capabilities, so by reducing the prices of the higher-memory variants of the Module 4, we strive to make these projects more accessible and economically viable. This price reduction aims to open up new possibilities for both enterprises and enthusiasts to innovate in ways beyond our current vision.
